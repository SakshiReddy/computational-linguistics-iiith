List of Functionalities 

1. DisplaySelectedWord() - Function to display word selected under Formed Sentence

2. ResetSentence() - Reset to original state when Re-form the sentence button is clicked

3. CheckCorrectness() - Based on rules evaluate whether sentence is correct

4. DisplayResult() - After checking the sentence’s correctness display whether it is right or wrong

5. InitialSelection() - For the first word selected display heading Formed Sentence etc

6. DisplayCorrectAnswer() - When Get Correct Sentence is selected, display the various right answers
